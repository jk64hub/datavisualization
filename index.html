Specialty Occupation H1 Visa Trends

Over the last few years, the US government was revising the immigration policies and issuing amendments to H1B visa programs with the intent to create higher wages and employment rates for US workers and protect economic interests.
H1B visa program allows US employers to temporarily employ foreign nationals with specialized knowledge in theory and practical applications. Employers across different sectors which include but not limited to the fields of Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Mathematics are impacted by these changes.
This visualizations displays the H1B visa trends in various sectors over time and the top ten employers in each sector.
